About Mario's

Our pizza business has been built over generations of our family history. Starting back in 1889 by our great ancestor Rafael who began making pizzas for his local villagers as a way of using up various ingredients that would otherwise go to waste. As time went by he was able to develop his own recipe for the pizza dough and the sauce. The technique and ingredients he used are a well kept family secret even to this day and we continue to create our pizza's with the same techniques and speciality that Rafael had developed all them years ago. Over the years, our pizzas have become a well known brand and in the last few years we have decided to share this experience with others around the world by opening new stores outside of italy, in the U.S.A.
